# Product

## Register
product

## Users
SCRB Karnataka (State Crime Record Bureau) command staff and field officers. Used in operational contexts (headquarters and out in the field).

## Product Purpose
To unify siloed crime data and provide real-time intelligence including hotspot detection, repeat-offender networks, and emerging trends to enable proactive policing and rapid decision-making.

## Brand Personality
Authoritative, clear, objective, and analytical. It must feel like a real, professional law enforcement tool.

## Anti-references
Avoid sci-fi movie tropes, enterprise bloatware, and frivolous animations. The interface should not feel like a toy or a generic consumer app.

## Design Principles
1. **Clarity over Flash**: Data must be instantly readable without decorative noise.
2. **Actionable Intelligence**: Every metric or alert must lead to an obvious next step.
3. **Objective Truth**: Visualizations must be honest and unbiased, presenting facts neutrally.
4. **Field Readiness**: Must remain functional and clear even in sub-optimal viewing conditions.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Must be readable in bright daylight (for field officers) and use color-blind safe palettes, especially for critical status indicators like risk badges.
